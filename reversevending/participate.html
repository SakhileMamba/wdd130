Join the Movement

We invite you to be a part of this transformative movement towards a more sustainable Eswatini. Whether you're an individual looking to recycle and earn rewards, an investor seeking impactful opportunities, a local community, government agency, or a stakeholder passionate about environmental issues, your involvement is crucial. Together, we can build a greener future by embracing the potential of Reverse Vending Machines and spreading the message of recycling across our beautiful nation.

Building and Deploying Reverse Vending Machines in Eswatini

Our vision is to establish a network of RVMs across Eswatini, promoting widespread recycling and environmental awareness. We aim to collaborate with you to implement this sustainable solution. The deployment of RVMs will include strategic placement in public areas, shopping centers, educational institutions, and other high-traffic locations. By making RVMs easily accessible, we can encourage broad participation and maximize the positive impact on our environment.

Support Our Cause

Your generous support can make a significant difference in our efforts to promote recycling and environmental sustainability in Eswatini. By making a donation, you contribute directly to the development and expansion of our reverse vending machine network. You also support educational programs, community outreach, and awareness campaigns that encourage recycling and responsible waste management. This all enables us to reach more communities and make a larger impact.

Environmental Impact and Sustainability Goals

By embracing RVMs and encouraging recycling, you can make significant strides towards achieving environmental sustainability. Through increased recycling rates, we can reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills, conserve energy, and minimize greenhouse gas emissions. By creating a culture of recycling, we can protect our natural resources, preserve biodiversity, and contribute to a healthier and cleaner Eswatini for future generations.
