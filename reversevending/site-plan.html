Eswatini Recycling Site Plan

Sakhile Mamba

WDD 130-C2

Overview

Purpose

To provide information introducing reverse vending machines for recycling to the people of Eswatini. The website will educate the general population about what reversing vending machines are in the context of encouraging people to recycle, how they work, and how they will be built and deployed in the country.

Audience

The website is targeted to all people of all ages, races and creeds. It is also targeted to investors, donors, and any other stakeholder with a focus on environmental issues.
